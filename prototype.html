Rules and edge cases

No magic quality oracle. Just explicit outcomes for money, timing, and file integrity.

Client goes quiet

After delivery, the 48-hour review clock starts. Silence triggers payout.

Outcome: 125 USDC to freelancer

Freelancer never delivers

Once the delivery deadline passes without a committed file hash, refund becomes claimable.

Outcome: 125 USDC to client

Client disputes once

Funds stay locked. The selected arbiter can pay, refund, or split the amount.

Outcome: arbiter resolves escrow

File is corrupt or missing

The ciphertext hash proves what was submitted. A mismatch blocks acceptance.

Outcome: dispute path opens

Client approves

The payout event becomes the release condition for the decryption key.

Outcome: payment released, original unlocked

After the unlock

The client owns the decrypted copy. Handshake does not pretend digital files cannot be copied.
